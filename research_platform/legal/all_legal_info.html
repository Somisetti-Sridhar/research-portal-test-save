Overview
The Research Portal is a student-developed, open-source academic platform created to facilitate research sharing and academic collaboration. It is strictly non-commercial and developed using open-source technologies. The platform provides an accessible environment for students, researchers, and educators to upload, explore, and collaborate on academic research content. It is built with privacy, transparency, and educational integrity at its core, and all functionality is designed with privacy-by-design principles.

Purpose and Mission
The platform is intended solely for educational and academic purposes. It supports the ethical sharing of research materials and promotes open access to scholarly content. The mission is to enhance academic collaboration while safeguarding user data and respecting research integrity.

Platform Ownership
The Research Portal is maintained by independent student developers. The primary developer and point of contact is Sridhar Somisetti at sridharsomisettiwork@gmail.com. Co-developers include Sairam Maruri at sairam.maruri@gmail.com, and Ram Charan Barma at ramcharanbarma99@gmail.com. All contributors are committed to maintaining user privacy and ensuring data security in line with best practices.

Data Collection and Use
User accounts may include data such as email addresses, usernames, affiliations, and optional profile information. This data is collected solely for authentication and academic networking purposes. User consent is required for data collection, and retention is limited to the active duration of the account plus one year. Users may upload research papers along with associated metadata and version history. This data is used to support academic sharing and collaboration. Ownership and control of uploaded content remain with the user, and such data is retained permanently unless removed by the user. AI-powered features on the platform utilize anonymized search queries and behavioral data to improve recommendation systems and interface functionality. If a user is using this platform, they have given consent.

Data Protection and Security
The platform employs modern technical and organizational security measures. HTTPS is enforced across the entire website, and passwords are securely hashed. Access to administrative functionality is role-restricted. Encrypted storage is used for all documents. Code reviews are conducted regularly to ensure compliance with privacy standards.

Privacy Principles
The platform adheres to several core privacy principles. All data is used solely for educational purposes. No personal data is exploited for commercial gain or advertising. The platform is open source and community-developed, and improvements are made exclusively to serve academic interests. Only essential data is collected, and no third-party trackers or advertising integrations are included. Users have full control over document visibility, and optional profile fields are not required. Users retain the right to access, correct, export, or delete their data at any time. All research uploads and account settings are fully user-controlled. Cookies are limited to essential session cookies required for secure login.

User Rights and Access
Users can access and export all personal data and uploaded research materials. Assistance with data migration is available upon request. Users also have the ability to correct personal information, delete accounts, or remove individual documents. The platform respects user control over their academic content and associated metadata.

International and National Compliance
The platform follows relevant international and national data protection frameworks. It is designed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 of India. Users have access to their personal data and may correct or delete their information where applicable. As an open-source, student-maintained academic platform, the system does not provide formal mechanisms for raising legal disputes or initiating cross-border data complaints. By using the platform, users acknowledge that it is non-commercial, community-driven, and does not offer structured legal support channels.

Third-Party Services and Infrastructure
The platform uses open-source technologies including Django, MySQL, SQLite, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. All hosting infrastructure providers are selected based on their compliance with applicable data protection laws. No user data is shared with any third-party services without explicit user consent. All third-party tools used are strictly for non-commercial, educational infrastructure support.

Content Sourcing and Licensing
The Research Portal hosts academic research papers obtained exclusively from open-access repositories and other public domain scholarly platforms. All documents made available through the platform are either open-access by license or released under terms that permit reuse and redistribution for educational purposes. No proprietary or restricted-access content is hosted or distributed. Users are expected to upload only content they have the legal right to share, and the platform enforces academic licensing integrity. All software tools and libraries used in development are retained under their original licenses. The platform codebase, including all enhancements, is released under the Apache License 2.0 to ensure transparency and encourage community development.

Risk Management
Platform risk is low due to its non-commercial mission, transparent codebase, and minimal data collection. AI functionalities are clearly disclosed, and users opt in with informed consent. All data handling practices conform to international compliance frameworks. Encrypted communication and secure authentication help reduce operational risk.
